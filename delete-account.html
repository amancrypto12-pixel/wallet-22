DPN Network Account Deletion

This page explains how DPN Network users can request deletion of their account and associated app data.

Request account deletion

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at support@dpn.network.
  2. Use the subject: DPN Network Account Deletion Request.
  3. Send the email address used for your DPN Network account.
  4. We may ask for confirmation to protect your account.
  5. After confirmation, your account deletion request will be processed.

Data deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account where deletion is technically available.
  • User profile data such as username, email, invite code, wallet address fields, and app preferences.
  • Simulator reward records, mining sessions, referrals, notifications, and related in-app activity data.

Data retained

Some records may be retained only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or operational logs. Retained records are limited and normally removed or anonymized within 90 days unless a longer period is legally required.

Partial data deletion

If you want some data deleted without deleting your account, email support@dpn.network and describe the data you want removed.
